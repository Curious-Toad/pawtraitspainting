Gallery

Pawtraits painting was set up in 2017 by watercolour artist Grace Roberts. Based in West Sussex the artist has easy access to the countryside and nature.

 

After studying various artistic disciplines in both City college of Brighton and Hove and the University of Brighton Grace Roberts moved back home to pursue painting full time and is a member of the Crawley Arts Society. This latest venture is in response to friends and fellow pet owners requesting paintings in her unique style.

​

Pawtraits paintings aims to capture your pets personality as well as a true likeness that you can cherish forever. It also makes a great gift!

Privacy & Cookies Policy

Privacy

Your Privacy is important to us. Like most websites, we use a few pieces of technology on this website to ensure we're delivering the best service possible. These technologies are nonintrusive and can be opted-out from if you wish.

 

On this website, we do not store any personal information other than what is required to process your order/request and which you provide via our contact form, email address or social media channels. No information will be passed on or sold to third-parties.

However, like most websites, this website uses some technologies from third-parties to provide optional services:

Cognito Forms

The Request A PAWtrait form is provided by Cognito Forms, a leading online tool to make building and maintaining contact forms simple. Any data entered into this form is securely stored on Cognito Form's servers.

If you do not wish to enter information here you may email your enquiry directly to pawtraitspainting@gmail.com.

MailChimp

Our newsletters are provided by MailChimp - an industry leader in email marketing tools. This website uses a form provided by MailChimp to allow visitors to easily sign up to our newsletter.

Unlike many email service providers, MailChimp only allows the use of "opted-in" data i.e. an individual must have given prior consent to be contacted. We will only contact you via our newsletter campaigns if you have subscribed to them. If you subscribe any details entered will be securely stored on MailChimp's servers.

Subscribing via our sign up page is optional; if you do not wish to be contacted via email newsletter campaigns simply do not subscribe.

If you have already subscribed and wish to be removed from our list(s) (unsubscribed), you can do so from the links at the bottom of every email or here.

Google Analytics

Like the majority of websites, this website uses Google Analytics to ensure we provide the best service possible. In practice, this means identifying any issues with the functionality of the website or areas that can be improved, such as page loading speed, broken pages etc.

All data tracked in Google Analytics is anonymous - no personally identifiable information is tracked. Google Analytics works by using cookies (small text files stored in your web browser). If you do not wish to use Google Analytics whilst using this website you may disable the cookies required by following the instructions here.

Cookies

This website uses cookies, including third-party cookies (for example Google Analytics), to help provide the best experience possible. Continued use of this site implies your acceptance of all cookies used by this website (including third-party ones). If you wish to continue to use this site without cookies then you can disable them in your web browser (please refer to the help link supplied by your browser), although you may find some areas of the site no longer work as intended.

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy or cookie usage, please contact pawtraitspainting@gmail.com.
